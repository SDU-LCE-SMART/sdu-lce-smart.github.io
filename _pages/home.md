### About the Lab

The **SMART Life Cycle Engineering Lab** at the **University of Southern Denmark** develops methods and digital tools for **sustainable resource management**.

Our work focuses on:
- circular economy systems
- industrial symbiosis
- life cycle engineering
- decision-support tools for sustainability

### Research Areas

**Circular Economy Systems**  
Designing systems that improve material reuse, reduce waste, and support sustainable transitions.

**Industrial Symbiosis**  
Studying how industries can exchange energy, materials, and by-products more effectively.

**Life Cycle Engineering**  
Applying life cycle thinking to products, systems, and resource strategies.

**Decision Support Tools**  
Developing optimization, simulation, and data-driven tools for sustainable planning.
