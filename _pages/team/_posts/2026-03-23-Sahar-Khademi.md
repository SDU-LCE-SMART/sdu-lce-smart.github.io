Sahar Khademi holds a PhD in Biosystems Engineering – Agricultural Machinery Design from Isfahan University of Technology (IUT). Since March 2023, she has been working as a Research Assistant on a remote collaborative project involving the Institute of Green Technology (IGT) and the Life Cycle Engineering Department (LCE) at the University of Southern Denmark (SDU), Odense, Denmark, as well as the Biogas Institute of the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs in China. Since January 2025, she has been affiliated with SDU, where she contributes to European-funded projects including ZDHydro, MARMADE, and ALLIANCE. Her research focuses on waste management, Power-to-X technologies, renewable energy systems, biorefineries, wastewater treatment, nutrient recovery, and prospective SSP-based life cycle assessment.
