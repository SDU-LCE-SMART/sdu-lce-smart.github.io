Hadis Marami holds a PhD in Biosystems Engineering – Renewable Energy from the University of Tehran. Since January 2023, she has been affiliated with the University of Southern Denmark (SDU), where she contributes to European-funded projects including TAKE-OFF (2023–2024) and Agro4Agri (2024–2027). Her research interests focus on Power-to-X technologies, e-fuels, renewable energy systems, biorefineries, wastewater treatment, nutrient recovery, process monitoring, Product Environmental Footprint (PEF) method, as well as techno-economic analysis and prospective SSP-based Life Cycle Assessment. <br>
